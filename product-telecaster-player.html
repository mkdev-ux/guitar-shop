Telecaster (Player Series)

The Telecaster Player Series brings classic Fender style and tone to modern players. Known for its punchy sound, smooth playability, and versatile performance, this Tele is ready for any stage.

Price: $700

Key Features

  • Player Series Alnico V Tele single-coil pickups for bright, punchy tones
  • Modern "C" maple neck for comfortable, fast playability
  • 6-saddle string-through-body bridge for solid tuning and sustain
  • 22 medium jumbo frets for smooth bending and extended range
  • Classic alder body with gloss polyester finish
