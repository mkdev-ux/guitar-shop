RG Series (Gio/Standard)

Affordable and reliable, the RG Series Gio is perfect for beginners and intermediate players seeking sharp, aggressive tones and fast playability.

Price: $300

Key Features

  • Poplar body for a balanced and bright tone
  • Maple neck with a thin "Wizard III" profile for fast playing
  • Rosewood fingerboard with 24 jumbo frets
  • Infinity R humbucker pickup for aggressive sound
  • Ibanez tremolo bridge for smooth vibrato effects
  • Chrome hardware
  • Available in multiple finishes
