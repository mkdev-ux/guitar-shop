AZ Series (Premium/Prestige)

The AZ Series blends classic style with modern playability. Crafted with premium woods and versatile pickups, these guitars deliver rich tones suitable for any genre. Ideal for professional musicians seeking both aesthetics and performance.

Price: $1,500

Key Features

  • Premium alder body with carved maple top
  • Wizard III Maple neck for fast playability
  • Versatile Seymour Duncan Hyperion pickups
  • Gotoh T1502 tremolo bridge for smooth vibrato
  • Modern 22-fret rosewood fingerboard with stainless steel frets

Specifications

Body Alder with Carved Maple Top
Neck Maple Wizard III
Fingerboard Rosewood, 22 Stainless Steel Frets
Pickups Seymour Duncan Hyperion (Neck & Bridge), Hyperion Single Coil (Middle)
Controls 1 Volume, 1 Tone, 5-Way Pickup Selector
Bridge Gotoh T1502 Tremolo Bridge
Finish Premium Gloss Transparent
Weight Approx. 8.1 lbs
