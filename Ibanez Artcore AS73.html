Ibanez Artcore AS73

This semi-hollow body guitar combines warm, jazzy tones with the playability of a solid body. Perfect for blues, jazz, and classic rock enthusiasts who want rich resonance and vintage style at an affordable price.

Price: $400

Key Features

  • Semi-hollow body for warm, resonant tone
  • Classic dual humbucker pickup configuration
  • Bound rosewood fingerboard for smooth playability
  • Artcore mahogany set-in neck for excellent sustain
  • Vintage-style F-holes and dual cutaway design

Specifications

Body Laminated Maple Top, Back & Sides
Neck Artcore Mahogany Set-in Neck
Fingerboard Bound Rosewood, 22 Medium Frets
Pickups Classic Elite Humbuckers (Neck & Bridge)
Controls 2 Volume, 2 Tone, 3-Way Pickup Selector
Bridge ART-1 Bridge with Stopbar Tailpiece
Finish Transparent Cherry Red
Weight Approx. 7.5 lbs
