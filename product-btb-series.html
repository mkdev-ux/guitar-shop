BTB Series

The BTB Series offers extended range, deep bass tones, and ergonomic design, perfect for bassists who love progressive and technical styles.

Price: $800

Key Features

  • Extended range 5 and 6-string bass options
  • Mahogany body with flamed maple top
  • Powerful Bartolini pickups for clarity and punch
  • Neck-through construction for enhanced sustain
  • Ergonomic body shape for comfortable playing

Specifications

Body Mahogany with Flamed Maple Top
Neck 5-piece Maple/Walnut Neck-Through
Fingerboard Rosewood, 24 Medium Frets
Pickups Bartolini MK-1 (Neck & Bridge)
Controls 3-Band EQ, Volume, Balance
Bridge Mono-Rail IV Bridge
Finish Gloss Transparent
Weight Approx. 9.5 lbs
