Gibson Les Paul Tribute

Designed with vintage tone in mind, the Tribute offers a lightweight mahogany body and burstbucker pickups for authentic Les Paul sounds. Its natural finish and robust build make it a standout at a budget-friendly price.

Price: $900

Key Features

  • Burstbucker 2 & 3 pickups for authentic vintage tone
  • Lightweight mahogany body for comfort
  • Maple top with satin nitrocellulose finish
  • SlimTaper neck profile for fast playability
  • Adjustable Nashville Tune-O-Matic bridge for stable tuning

Specifications

Body Mahogany with Maple Top
Neck Maple, SlimTaper Profile
Fingerboard Rosewood, 22 Medium Jumbo Frets
Pickups Burstbucker 2 (neck), Burstbucker 3 (bridge)
Controls 2 Volume, 2 Tone, 3-Way Pickup Selector
Bridge Nashville Tune-O-Matic with Stopbar Tailpiece
Finish Satin Iced Tea
Weight Approx. 9 lbs
